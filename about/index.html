Arpan Dhatt / About

I'm Arpan, a freshman at UT Austin Computer Science. I occasionally post to this website, which usually happens to be CS-focused or at least CS-related. I spent a lot of time (read: too much time) making micro-optimizations to this site to make it easier on the eyes, just for readers like you!

But anyway, I've worked on projects in various fields of software engineering. During high school I explored game development with Unity and C#, machine learning with Python, and iOS development with Swift. However, I currently mostly focus on computer architecture, and parallel programming. In my off-time, I read about comp. arch. and optimization. Currently, I'm reading through Computer Systems: A Programmers Perspective with a hint of Introduction to Computing Systems from Bits, Gates, to C to round it off. I also work on an LC3 virtual machine which you can view on my github repository. If you have any reading suggestions, don't keep them to yourself. Let me know!

Outside of academics, I casually play Ultimate Frisbee® and Ping Pong. I'm not particularly good at either, relatively speaking, but that doesn't stop me from having fun.
